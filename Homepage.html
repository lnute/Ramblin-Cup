Since the opening of Georgia Tech in 1888, we have established numerous traditions, most of which continue to this day. As Yellow Jackets, we've all experienced them from the second we've step foot on the campus, giving all of us our deep sense of school pride from day one. Being the root of our community, the Ramblin' Cup's theme this year is, of course, Georgia Tech Traditions.
