Rules

Eligibility
     - Must be a currently enrolled undergraduate student at Georgia Institute of Technology
     - Should not have been eliminated from the Ramblin’ Cup for any reason

Participating
     - Must sign up officially through website in order to participate, officially gain points for team, and enable tracking of participation
     - Can only participate as a member of your overall team. Teams for specific events can be a mix of members from different teams

Points
     - Will be logged by an official facilitating the event
     - Points are fully awarded to the winning teams
     - In the case of mixed teams, points will be evenly distributed
     - Current standings of teams are regularly updated on the homepage

Submitting an event
     - Event must be fully organized prior to submission
     - Must allow all students to be eligible
     - Must last only as long as the current Ramblin’ Cup period

Prize
     - Prize is announced at the beginning of every school year (August) on homepage
     - Prize is awarded at the end of the year (April)
     - Only those who participated in at least one event is eligible to receive the prize

Elimination
     - Not adhering to rules can result in permanent elimination from the Ramblin' Cup

All participants and events must also adhere to institute rules and regulations as well.

For more information or questions, please view our FAQ and Help page.
